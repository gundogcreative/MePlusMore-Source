Terms

MePlusMore is strongly committed to the privacy of all its users. We want you to always be confident that your MePlusMore experience is safe and secure. If you have any additional privacy or security questions after reading this information, please contact us. This Privacy Policy governs how we collect and use your information when you use our websites, mobile apps, and related services ("Services").
What kind of information does MePlusMore collect from me?

We collect and use the following information: Information you give us. We collect the information you give to us when you are using our Services, including the names and email addresses of members. Usage Information. We collect information from and about your use of the Services and the devices you use to access the Services. This includes things like IP addresses, the type of browser and device you use, the web page you visited before coming to our sites, and information about the advertisements you view and the links you click on. Your devices (depending on your settings) may also transmit location information and/or an advertising identifier to the Services.

Payment information.

If you provide us with any payment information related to the Services, e.g., if you upgrade to MePlusMore Gold from our website, we will ask for your credit card information, which will be processed directly by a Company Affiliate or a Vendor, as each are defined below.

What does MePlusMore do with my information?

We use the information we collect to provide, personalize, improve, offer, and protect our Services. If you contact MePlusMore, we may use your information to provide customer support to you. We may use your email address to communicate with you, for example, to inform you when a subscription is ending.MePlusMore is able to provide many of our Services free of charge because we are supported by advertising. In order to make these services available, we may use the information we collect to improve the advertisements you see by making them more relevant.

With whom does MePlusMore share my information?

WE UNDERSTAND YOUR INFORMATION IS VERY IMPORTANT TO YOU, AND WE TREAT IT CAREFULLY. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share your personal information with third parties, except as described below.With vendors working for MePlusMore. MePlusMore uses certain trusted third parties to help us provide, personalize, improve, offer and protect our Services ("Vendors"). Vendors will access your information only to perform tasks on our behalf, in compliance with this Privacy Policy.

For legal reasons. We may disclose your information to third parties if we determine that such disclosure is necessary to (a) comply with the law or in response to a law enforcement agency's request; (b) protect any person from death or serious bodily injury; (c) prevent fraud or abuse of MePlusMore or our users; or (d) protect MePlusMore’s property rights.

What about third party links?

MePlusMore does not have control over the privacy policies of advertisers or other third-party websites. There are links to other websites and pages provided by third parties on MePlusMore. Similarly, advertisements typically link to third-party websites. An advertiser may receive the information you enter into a form or survey contained in an advertisement. Any personal information you enter is provided directly to that third party and is subject to that third party's privacy policy.

Do you use Cookies?

Cookies and other technologies. We use technologies like cookies and pixel tags to provide, improve, protect and promote our Services. For example, cookies help us with things like remembering your account for your next visit, understanding how you are interacting with our Services, and improving them based on that information. Cookies can also be used to target advertisements to you. To learn more about this practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, click here. MePlusMore may not recognize the “do not track” signals that some browsers may employ. You can set your browser to not accept cookies, but this may limit your ability to use the Services.

How does MePlusMore protect children's information?

MePlusMore takes protection of your family's personal information very seriously. MePlusMore will never knowingly request personally identifiable information or private content from anyone under the age of 13. In order to register and create a MePlusMore account you must be 13 years of age or older. You may, at any time, direct MePlusMore to delete all identifiable information regarding you or your family that you have provided or that has been collected from your family. To do so, please contact our Privacy Officer at the contact information below. However, if you do any of the foregoing, you and your family will not be able to use the MePlusMore account or our Services.

How do I access or correct my information collected by MePlusMore?

As a MePlusMore user, you have the opportunity to review and modify your personally identifiable information and your private content any time you log onto your MePlusMore account using an email address and password listed on the account.

What if I live outside of the United States?

MePlusMore’s Services are hosted in the United States. If you are visiting our website from outside the United States, you are transferring your personal data into the United States when you use the MePlusMore service. Many regions, such as the European Union, have data collection protections that differ from U.S. law. If you are a European Union citizen, please see our Safe Harbor Privacy Policy.

What if our business is sold?

We reserve the right to transfer any information we have about you in the event of a merger with a third party or upon a sale or transfer of all or a portion of our business or assets to a third party acquirer. The new company would be required to protect all the information that MePlusMore has collected from its users under the same terms of this Privacy Policy.

How does MePlusMore maintain security?

MePlusMore is strongly committed to protecting your information. MePlusMore uses industry standard methods of securing user information and data. Examples of our security measures include, where appropriate, firewalls and secure socket layers. We also maintain strict limits on who at MePlusMore can access the information databases that contain your information. Since there is no such thing as perfect security over the Internet, we cannot guarantee 100% security. However, in the unlikely case of a security breach on our databases, we will take immediate steps to resolve the problem swiftly and effectively.

What happens if I want to forward my MePlusMore information to a 3rd party?

If you use one of the MePlusMore features designed to allow you to voluntarily forward or share your information or content with a 3rd party outside of your secure account, we will forward or share access to such content from your account to the individual’s email address as you provided it to us. Such email address may be stored by MePlusMore for your future use but will not be used by MePlusMore to initiate any emails. By sharing your content, you automatically limit MePlusMore’s ability to keep your content private. We recommend that you do this only with trusted 3rd party contacts.

Will there be changes and updates to this Privacy Policy?

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. When we do, we will also revise the "last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy from time to time to stay informed of any updates or changes to this Privacy Policy. If you continue to use our Services after we make changes to the Privacy Policy, you agree to the changes.

How do I contact MePlusMore?
